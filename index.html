About Kents

Located in Kirinya, Bweyogerere. Kents Beverages Limited makes juice from organically grown Ugandan Bananas (ndiizi). The juice is made available in 350/500 mL bottles. The juice is 100% organic as neither sugar nor preservatives are added to it.
